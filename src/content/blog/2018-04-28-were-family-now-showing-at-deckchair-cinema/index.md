Nathaniel Kelly's debut feature film, which I had several key roles on, will be screening thrice at Darwin's wonderful outdoor Deckchair Cinema in May.

![](./maxresdefault.jpg)

We're Family now is about a lovable little suburban family. It was shot entirely in Darwin.

Check it out on <a href="https://www.deckchaircinema.com/films/family-now-presented-nathaniel-kelly/">12 May</a> (with Q&amp;A), <a href="https://www.deckchaircinema.com/films/were-family-now/">18 May</a> or <a href="https://www.deckchaircinema.com/films/were-family-now/">26 May</a>.
